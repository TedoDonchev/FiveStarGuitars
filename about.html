About Us

Five-Star Guitars was launched in 2009, and has become one of the largest retailers of musical instruments and equipment in Bulgaria, now having over 2.7 million registered customers.

Over the years we have grown in all aspects — and continue to every day — but our goals have remained the same. We are now one of the fastest growing music companies in Europe.

Our Mission is simple: We want to make purchasing and owning musical instruments and equipment easy for everyone, by providing great prices and excellent service, regardless of where you live, or how you buy from us.

We supply musical instruments and equipment manufactured by many of the industry's best known brands, at very competitive prices. We also produce a range of fantastic value, high quality own-brand music products.
